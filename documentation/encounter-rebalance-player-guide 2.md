# Encounter Rebalance Player Guide (Johto)

This guide summarizes the wild encounter rebalance focused on making Johto species, babies, and select variants available during the main story.

## What Changed

- Johto species are available earlier so teams are less Kanto-heavy in early game.
- Several Johto species previously locked late/postgame were moved into Johto routes/caves.
- Regional/convergent additions were placed by ecology/lore theme.
- Baby Pokemon were distributed in safer early/mid-game areas.
- Regional forms in wild tables are now encoded using form slots (internal change; no gameplay action needed).

## Early-Game & Mid-Game Highlights

### Route 32
- `Paldean Wooper`: **15% all day** (walking)
- `Mantyke`: **5% morning/day** (walking)
- `Azurill`: **5% day** (walking)
- Water support is also available in surf/rod tables.

### Union Cave
- 1F walking: `Azurill` added (day)
- B2F walking: `Alolan Sandshrew` increased to a real encounter presence

### Route 33
- `Hisuian Voltorb`: boosted in day encounters

### Ilex Forest
- Day table includes `Hisuian Voltorb` and `Galarian Farfetch'd`
- Night remains a danger zone with dark/ice pressure options

### Route 37 / Burned Tower
- `Hisuian Growlithe` appears in Route 37 and Burned Tower ecosystem
- Burned Tower night includes urban-pollution variant pressure (`Alolan Grimer`)

### Route 42 + Mt. Mortar 1F
- `Hisuian Sneasel`: **5% morning/day**

### Route 46
- `Hisuian Sneasel` removed from this route and relocated (see above)

## Baby Pokemon Access

- `Mantyke` and `Azurill` are available on-foot in Route 32 (and related cave corridor) to avoid forcing Surf/Rod progression for these babies.

## Urban / Invasive Variant Theme

### Routes 38/39 (farm + trade corridor)
- Day: `Galarian Meowth`
- Night: `Alolan Meowth`
- Route 39 day now supports ranch identity with `Doduo` + `Dodrio` mix

### National Park
- `Galarian Ponyta`: morning/day
- `Eevee`: **1% morning/day** (escaped pet concept)

### Sprout/Bell Tower night tables
- `Alolan Rattata` integrated as nocturnal invasive pest encounters

## Deep Ecology / Micro-Climate Theme

### Ice Path (multiple floors)
- `Alolan Vulpix`: all-day integration
- `Galarian Mr. Mime`: all-day integration

### Mt. Mortar deep ecosystem
- `Alolan Geodude` / `Alolan Diglett`: deep-level magnetic mutation theme

### Slowpoke Well
- `Galarian Slowpoke` integrated in deep-well ecosystem logic

### Whirl Islands (deep water / deep caves)
- `Hisuian Qwilfish` and `Galarian Corsola` integrated into high-pressure marine tables

## Player Notes

- The rebalance is designed to increase team-building options before major Johto boss spikes.
- If you are hunting specific forms, use time-of-day checks and area-specific tables.
- Rare slots are still rare, but key lines are no longer gated as hard as vanilla.
