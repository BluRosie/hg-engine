# Result-Based EXP (Player Guide)

## What this system changes
Battles now reward more than just winning.

Your Pokemon can gain extra EXP for:
- Dealing damage.
- Causing certain residual damage effects.
- Reducing enemy stats (with stronger rewards for more impactful drops).

## Base EXP still exists
You still receive normal EXP from defeating Pokemon.

On top of that, bonus EXP is added based on what your Pokemon contributed during the fight.

## What counts as contribution
- **Damage dealt**: The more HP your Pokemon removes, the more bonus it can earn.
- **Residual damage**: Effects like seeded/trapping-style damage can add contribution when your Pokemon is the source.
- **Stat drops**: Lowering enemy stats can add bonus EXP.

## Why some stat drops give more bonus than others
Not all stat changes are equally valuable.

- Defensive drops (Defense / Sp. Defense) generally convert strongly into bonus because they directly make future damage easier.
- Offensive/Speed/Accuracy/Evasion drops also count, but use different balance weights.

## Team play
Different party members can earn bonus EXP based on their own contribution.

This means support Pokemon that help secure KOs can still be rewarded.

## Important note
This is a balance feature. Exact values are tuned by the hack's settings, so bonus EXP can vary between versions.
